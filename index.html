Angel Laxamana

Sophomore, UPLB

Born on October 24, 2003, at Romana Pangan District Hospital, Angel Alzona Laxamana hails from a family of five. Her father is from Pampanga, while her mother is from the south, specifically Binan, Laguna. Their fateful meeting has blossomed into a tightly-knit family. The oldest is a registered civil engineer, the middle child is pursuing a degree in BS AAMT, and Angel is the youngest. She is currently a sophomore at the University of the Philippines Los Baños. Hard work and patience are her keys to putting in the effort to complete a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science. She grew up in Floridablanca, Pampanga, a small town that is now experiencing growth into a wealthier area. This small town holds a story about a quiet but ambitious woman.
